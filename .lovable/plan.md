

# Inspector Himanshu Singh Rajawat — Personal Brand Website

A modern, cinematic single-page scrolling website showcasing the "Singham of Rajasthan" — a police officer turned filmmaker with an extraordinary story of resilience.

## Design Direction
- **Style:** Cinematic, professional, inspirational — inspired by Apple product pages
- **Colors:** Navy blue (#1a365d) + Deep gold (#d97706) with saffron accents
- **Typography:** Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body (Inter)
- **Animations:** Smooth scroll-triggered fade-ins, parallax backgrounds, animated stat counters

## Page Sections (Single Scrolling Page)

### 1. Hero Section
Full-screen cinematic opening with name, "The Singham of Rajasthan" tagline, and roles (Police Officer • Filmmaker • Motivational Speaker). Dark overlay, animated text entrance, scroll-down indicator.

### 2. Introduction
Centered, clean text block telling the core story — from village to vindication — in a few powerful lines. Quote-style formatting.

### 3. Journey Timeline
Vertical animated timeline from 1980s to 2026 covering key milestones: joining police, STF selection, custody years, degrees earned in prison, acquittal, CID posting, and Sagwaan film release. Items animate in on scroll.

### 4. Dual Identity (Split Screen)
50/50 split layout — Police career (navy blue background) on the left, Film career (deep red/cinematic background) on the right. Achievements, roles, and quotes for each identity.

### 5. The Prison University
Dark-themed section highlighting 3 degrees earned during 7+ years in custody. Powerful quote: "Those 7 years didn't break me. They made me." Visual transition from dark to light.

### 6. Achievements Grid
4-box grid showcasing Police Excellence, Legal Victory, Academic Achievement, and Cinematic Success. Hover effects, icons, and key stats.

### 7. Sagwaan Film Showcase
Cinematic section with movie details (IMDb 7.2/10, cast, crew), YouTube trailer embed placeholder, and call-to-action buttons (Watch Trailer, IMDb, BookMyShow).

### 8. Recognition & Media
Media logos, press coverage highlights, speaking engagements, and the "Super Cop" / "Singham of Rajasthan" titles.

### 9. Inspirational Quote
Large elegant typography featuring his personal philosophy on resilience and justice. Minimal, clean design.

### 10. Police Mitra Initiative
Community engagement section with mission points, impact description, and warm community-focused design.

### 11. Contact Section
Contact form with name, email, category dropdown (Media / Events / Police Mitra / Film / Other), and message. Social media icon links. Note about security protocols.

### 12. Footer
Minimal dark footer with name, titles, copyright, and disclaimer.

## Key Interactive Features
- Floating navigation dots on the right side for section jumping
- Scroll-triggered animations throughout
- Animated stat counters (7+ years, 3 degrees, 7.2 IMDb rating)
- Parallax background effects on hero and key sections
- Hover lift effects on achievement cards
- Responsive design (desktop, tablet, mobile)

## Technical Notes
- All images will use placeholders initially — ready for photo replacement later
- Contact form will work client-side (no backend needed initially)
- SEO meta tags and Open Graph tags included
- Fully responsive, mobile-first approach
